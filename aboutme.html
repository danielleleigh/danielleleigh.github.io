About Me

Leaving High School, I had no idea what I wanted to do in life. I joined the military in February of 2009, as an Aviation Ordnanceman doing bomb assembly. Upon exiting the military, I enrolled in college for a degree in Chemistry. After switching my degree path 2-3 times, I settled on Sociology, and obtained my degree in 2020.


Unfortunately, I quickly realized that Sociology and Social Work weren't for me and within a year of graduating I was searching for something new. Throughout my search, my sister constantly reminded me of how she believe I'd be great at development, especially since she knows I absolutely LOVED coding people's Myspace pages back in the day. In September of 2021, I enrolled in a HTML/CSS basics course and shortly after I was approved for the VA's VRRAP program, and shortly after I enrolled in DevCodeCamp. Now, upon completion of this program, I will be Full Stack Developer!
